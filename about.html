About NEUbie – A Student-Led Business Project from NEU

Our core mission is to design, produce, and distribute high-quality NEU-branded merchandise that strengthens school spirit and provides meaningful souvenirs for students, alumni, and visitors. From apparel and accessories to stationery and lifestyle items, every product reflects the pride and identity of the NEU community. By supporting NEUbie, you’re not only purchasing a memorable item—you’re also empowering the next generation of Vietnamese business leaders.

NEUbie is proudly founded and operated by students of the National Economics University. It serves as a real-world entrepreneurial platform where students apply business theories into practice. Every product, campaign, and decision reflects the creativity, passion, and leadership of young minds.

History of NEUbie

NEUbie is a dynamic student-run business project under the Faculty of Business Management at the National Economics University (NEU), Hanoi. Founded and operated by NEU students, NEUbie functions as a real-world entrepreneurial platform that combines academic knowledge with practical business experience.
