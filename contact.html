NEUbie is a student-run business project from the National Economics University (NEU), offering official NEU-themed merchandise such as apparel, accessories, and stationery. We aim to spread school spirit while providing practical business experience for students.

You can purchase our products directly through our official Facebook fan page or at on-campus pop-up booths during special events. Stay tuned to our page for updates on sales locations and new collections.

Currently, NEUbie offers standard product designs, but we occasionally accept group or bulk orders (e.g., for clubs or events). Please contact us directly via Facebook or the contact form for more details.

You can message us via our official Facebook fanpage, or fill out the contact form provided on our page. Our team will respond as soon as possible to assist you.
